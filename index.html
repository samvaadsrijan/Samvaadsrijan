Welcome to Samvaad Srijan Samiti

Samvaad Srijan Samiti is the official debating and intellectual forum of Rashtrakavi Ramdhari Singh Dinkar College of Engineering, Begusarai. We aim to foster critical thinking, articulate expression, and a culture of informed dialogue among students. Established with the spirit of participative democracy and youth empowerment, we organize debates, quizzes, elocutions, and thought-driven events throughout the academic year.

Join us in our mission to ignite minds and elevate conversations. Whether you are a thinker, a speaker, or simply curious — Samvaad has a space for every voice.
