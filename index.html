Welcome to Samvaad Srijan Samiti

Samvaad Srijan Samiti is the official debating and intellectual forum of Rashtrakavi Ramdhari Singh Dinkar College of Engineering, Begusarai. We aim to foster critical thinking, articulate expression, and a culture of informed dialogue among students.

We organize debates, quizzes, elocutions, and thought-driven events. Join us in our mission to ignite minds and elevate conversations.
